“ In the Summertime when the weather is high, You can stretch right up and touch the sky ”


Quark Summer Technical Projects (QSTP) in association with GDG (Google Developer Group) & WTM (Women Techmakers) Goa , is back in its 8th edition with a total of 21 projects spanning varied areas of study. For the first time, QSTP is offering projects in fields like Game Development, Data Science, Product Management among others. Being conducted in a completely online mode, QSTP offers a great opportunity to learn and interact with the experienced mentors from BITS Pilani, Goa at the convenience of one's own.


1. QSTP is Open for participation for Students from any College across the Nation irrespective of their year of study and all projects are Free of Cost.

2. Completion of the project/course will award the participant with a 'Certificate of Completion'.Moreover, the top participants in each course shall be awarded with a 'Certificate of Excellence'. Certificates will be issued by Quark 2023 in association with GDG & WTM Goa and certified by a BITS faculty member.

3. Last Date of Registration for the Projects is 4th July 2021(11:59 PM IST).
