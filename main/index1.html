Quark Summer Technical Projects (QSTP) is back in its 6th edition with a total of 25 projects spanning varied areas of study. For the first time, QSTP is offering projects in fields like Quantum Computing, Basics of Flight and Mechatronics among others. Being conducted in a completely online mode, QSTP offers a great opportunity to learn and interact with the experienced mentors from BITS Goa at the convenience of one's own. Completion of the project/course will award the participant with a 'Certificate of Completion'. Moreover, the top participants in each course shall be awarded with a 'Certificate of Excellence'.

All of these are offered free of charge to students of any year of study and in any college across India.
